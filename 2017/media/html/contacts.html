Alexandra Cristea
alexandra.i.cristea@durham.ac.uk
Alexandra works on a variety of topics centred on Adaptive Systems and Hypermedia, including AI and Education. Her research includes learner analytics, user modelling and personalisation, semantic web, social web and authoring, with over 250 papers on these subjects.
